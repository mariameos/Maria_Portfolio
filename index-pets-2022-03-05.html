    Signature 2€ coin

    Bank of Estonia

    The two-euro commemorative coin dedicated to the Estonian national animal, the wolf, will use a design by the artist Maria Meos. In total 60 designs were submitted for the international competition. The Eesti Pank Supervisory Board decided that Maria's design, was the best submitted.

    The signature two-euro commemorative coin dedicated to the Estonian national animal, the wolf, entered into circulation on 20 October 2021.
